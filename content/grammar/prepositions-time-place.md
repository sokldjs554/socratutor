# 시간·장소 전치사 (Prepositions: at / on / in)

## 형태
at / on / in + 명사

## 핵심 개념
범위가 **좁을수록 at, 중간이면 on, 넓을수록 in**.
- 시간: at (시각) < on (요일·날짜) < in (월·연도·계절)
- 장소: at (지점) < on (표면·선) < in (공간 내부)

## 용법
1. 시간: **at** 3 o'clock / **on** Monday, on July 5th / **in** March, in 2026, in summer
2. 장소: **at** the bus stop / **on** the table, on the 3rd floor / **in** the room, in Seoul
3. 주의: **at** night, **in** the morning/afternoon/evening

## 자주 하는 실수
- ❌ in Monday → ✅ **on** Monday
- ❌ on 2026 → ✅ **in** 2026
- ❌ I'll arrive to Seoul. → ✅ I'll arrive **in** Seoul. (arrive at/in — arrive to는 없음)
- next/last/this/every 앞에는 전치사 생략: ❌ on next Monday → ✅ next Monday
