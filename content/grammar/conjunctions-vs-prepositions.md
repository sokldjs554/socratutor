# 접속사 vs 전치사 (Conjunctions vs Prepositions)

## 형태
- 접속사 + 주어 + 동사 (절)
- 전치사 + 명사(구)

## 핵심 개념
의미가 같아도 **뒤에 오는 것이 절이면 접속사, 명사면 전치사**를 골라야 한다.
토익 파트5 최다 빈출 유형 중 하나.

## 용법 (짝으로 암기)
| 의미 | 접속사 (+절) | 전치사 (+명사) |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| ~동안 | while | during |
| ~때문에 | because | because of / due to |
| ~에도 불구하고 | although / though | despite / in spite of |

1. **While** I was staying in Paris, it rained. / **During** my stay in Paris, it rained.
2. **Because** the traffic was heavy, ... / **Because of** the heavy traffic, ...
3. **Although** it was raining, ... / **Despite** the rain, ...

## 자주 하는 실수
- ❌ During I was sleeping → ✅ **While** I was sleeping (뒤가 절이면 while)
- ❌ Despite of the rain → ✅ **Despite** the rain / In spite **of** the rain (despite에는 of 없음)
- ❌ Because of it was late → ✅ **Because** it was late
